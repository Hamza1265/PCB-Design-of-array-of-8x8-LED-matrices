We reserve rights for commercialization of Our product, even any integral part of this product can’t be commercialized without performing the agreement. It can’t be used in any commercial process, even not as a reference in the industry.

Cat-A.1: Integral parts:

Any part of code, schematic, PCB design, hardware design.

Cat-B: Use for Academic Purposes:

We strongly support innovation and bringing up hidden creativity. It would be an honor for us if our work is used for reference. But along with this we also have a strong sense of protection rights our product. Therefore we defined guidelines for the “How to use it” process.

How to use it

Non-Disclosure Agreement

Users have to perform a non-disclosure agreement with us

Can only be used as Reference

No user can’t exploit it with the copy and paste approach. Any part of the design ( i-e, code, schematic, PCB design) can’t be copied and used but only as a reference. In the above-restricted domain, it is important to note that users have to perform agreement along with their respective institutions not as an individual.
